VinylVault - Required API Keys & External Integrations
Generated: 2025-01-01

This app runs entirely client-side. All API keys are stored in your browser's
localStorage (synced from the app's KV storage layer). No server-side
environment variables are required.

To configure: Open the app → Settings tab → paste your keys.
